# Privacy Policy for Sus Night

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Sus Night ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **Display Name**: When you join a game, you choose a display name that other players can see. This name is not linked to any personal identity.

### Information Collected Automatically

- **Anonymous User ID**: We generate a random anonymous identifier to manage your game sessions and purchases. This ID is not linked to your real identity.

- **Gameplay Data**: We collect game-related data including:
  - Game room participation
  - Voting records within games
  - Round results and scores

- **Purchase Information**: If you make in-app purchases, we store a record of your entitlements (such as premium word packs or ad removal) to provide you access to purchased content.

### Information We Do NOT Collect

- Email addresses
- Phone numbers
- Location data
- Contact lists
- Photos or media
- Device identifiers for tracking purposes

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

1. **Provide Game Functionality**: Enable multiplayer gameplay, track game progress, and display player names during sessions.

2. **Manage Purchases**: Verify and restore your in-app purchases across devices.

3. **Improve the App**: Understand general usage patterns to improve game features (aggregated, non-personal data only).

## Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted cloud database provider. We implement industry-standard security measures including:

- Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
- Row-level security policies
- Secure authentication tokens

## Third-Party Services

### Supabase
We use Supabase for data storage and authentication. Their privacy policy can be found at: https://supabase.com/privacy

### Google AdMob
We display advertisements through Google AdMob. Google may collect certain information for ad personalization. You can learn more about Google's practices at: https://policies.google.com/privacy

### Apple App Store / Google Play Store
In-app purchases are processed through the respective app stores. Please refer to their privacy policies for information about purchase data handling.

## Data Retention

- **Gameplay Data**: Game room data is automatically deleted after games conclude or rooms expire (typically within 24 hours of inactivity).

- **Purchase Records**: Purchase entitlements are retained as long as you use the App to ensure you maintain access to purchased content.

- **Anonymous User ID**: Retained until you delete the app or request data deletion.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access**: Request information about data we hold about you.
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your data by contacting us.
- **Opt-out of Personalized Ads**: Use your device settings to limit ad tracking.

## Children's Privacy

Sus Night is suitable for all ages (4+). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The anonymous nature of our data collection means no personally identifiable information is gathered from users of any age.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the App and updating the "Last Updated" date.
